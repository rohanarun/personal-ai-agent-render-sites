FAQ for founder-led proof ops.

Is this a CRM?

No. The CRM remains the account system. Proof ops is the execution layer that prepares evidence and approved follow-up.

What is the first workflow to launch?

Start with buyer proof requests from calls, founder DMs, and SMS threads because the source context is fresh and high value.

How do you avoid thin SEO pages?

Make every page explain a real workflow, show practical criteria, and link only where the reader needs deeper context.

What should never be automated blindly?

Customer-facing claims. The agent should prepare sources and drafts while a human approves the promise.
