Super vs CRM reminders for founder follow-up.

CRM reminders tell a founder that a follow-up is due. Super can help prepare the work behind that follow-up: message context, account research, draft replies, browser routines, and approval state.

The gap is preparation, not reminders.

A CRM reminder is useful when the team already knows what to say. Founder-led follow-up often needs more: the last promise, the buyer's current context, a draft that matches founder tone, and a clear approval step.

CRM reminders store the obligation.

They keep the pipeline from disappearing, but the founder still has to reconstruct the message and context.
