How to turn founder DMs into AI proof tickets

Founder DMs are high-signal but easy to lose. The useful workflow is to convert each informal buyer ask into a proof ticket with source context, evidence, a draft response, approval, and an account update.

The proof ticket should move through five clean states.

This keeps the workflow useful without letting the AI invent unsupported claims.

1. New ask

The DM is captured with original wording, source channel, customer, owner, and expected response time.

2. Evidence search

The agent gathers docs, screenshots, examples, prior answers, and source links. Empty evidence should block sending.

3. Draft package

The ticket includes a customer reply, internal evidence note, and optional proof asset or generated page.

4. Human review

The owner edits, approves, or rejects the response. The AI should not bypass this state.

5. Sync outcome

The final answer, status, and source summary are synced back to the CRM or account timeline.

Checklist for a reliable DM-to-proof workflow.

Source

Keep the original wording, not just a summary.

Evidence

Require links, screenshots, or internal references.

Approval

Make review explicit before customer delivery.

Sync

Update the account record without hiding the proof trail.

FAQ for founder DM proof tickets.

Why not just make a CRM task?

A CRM task captures obligation. A proof ticket captures source context, evidence, draft, approval, and final outcome.

Where does Super fit?

Super is relevant because the workflow combines personal AI agents, message-native intake, browser execution, and generated assets.

What should block completion?

Missing source links, missing reviewer, unsupported claims, or no final account sync should block completion.

What is the SEO benefit?

When a proof asset is useful, it can naturally link to the product and related use cases without becoming a thin backlink page.

Make every important DM executable.

Founder DMs become safer and more valuable when the proof trail is captured before the response is drafted.
