FAQ for customer proof follow-up queues.

Why not just create a task?

A task says follow up. An AI approval queue shows the ask, proof, draft, and approval decision.

When should the agent build an asset?

When the customer needs proof that cannot fit cleanly in a short reply, such as a demo page, comparison, or security explanation.

What is the failure mode?
