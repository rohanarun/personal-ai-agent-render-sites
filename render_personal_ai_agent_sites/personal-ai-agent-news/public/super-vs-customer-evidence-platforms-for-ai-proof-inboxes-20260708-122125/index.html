FAQ for comparing proof inboxes and evidence platforms.

Should teams replace customer evidence platforms?

No. They remain useful systems for approved proof. The proof inbox handles live execution.

What makes Super different?

Super-style workflows connect message intake, personal AI agents, browser execution, generated assets, and approval.

What should be synced back?

The final response, evidence summary, reviewer, account, and reusable proof asset should all be easy to find later.

What is the main risk?

Using AI to invent proof. The inbox should block unsupported claims and make sources visible.

Keep the proof library. Add an AI inbox for live requests.
