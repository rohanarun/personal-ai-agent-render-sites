What the approval queue should show.

The interface should make the decision easy: what happened, why it matters, what the agent recommends, and what the founder can safely approve.

Person and source

Show the contact, thread, meeting note, or browser context that created the queue item.

Promise and urgency

Explain the commitment, due window, revenue or relationship importance, and uncertainty level.

Draft and evidence

Prepare a reply with supporting links, proof, references, or generated assets attached.

Approval controls

Approve, edit, snooze, research more, delegate, or archive. The workflow should never hide the human decision.

FAQ for founder AI approval queues.

Is this different from task management?

Yes. A task manager stores a reminder. An approval queue stores source context, recommendation, draft, and review state.

Should the agent send automatically?

Not at first. The safer path is preparation plus approval, then deeper autonomy only for narrow trusted cases.
